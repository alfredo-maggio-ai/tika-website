Our Story

The problem became urgent.
The technology finally caught up.

Tika was founded by serial entrepreneurs with deep experience in enterprise infrastructure, cloud security, and the systems that move data at scale.

For years, we watched enterprises struggle with the same unsolved problem: once sensitive data crosses a boundary — to a contractor, a cloud tool, a partner environment, or an AI system — control effectively disappears. Traditional DLP can see the door. It can't follow the data through it.

The enabling technology — cryptographic data controls, policy engines that work at the data layer, AI-aware access models — has finally matured. Tika is built at that intersection.

We're operating in stealth, advised by industry leaders, and preparing for a limited beta with security teams at enterprises that move sensitive data across boundaries every day.

Our Vision

Data free to move — without sacrificing security or governance.

In the modern enterprise, data creates its highest value when it can reach the right actor at the right moment. It should cross boundaries without losing security or governance. That's the problem we exist to solve.

Value in the right hands

Data delivers outcomes when it reaches the people and AI systems that can act on it. Access creates value. Unnecessary restriction destroys it.

Frictionless by default

Sharing shouldn't require heroics, training, or perfect behavior. Security that depends on people doing the right thing every time is security that will fail.

Security travels with the work

Protection holds as data moves across teams, companies, devices, and tools. The boundary is not the last line of defense — the data itself is.

Trust you can prove

Clear boundaries and auditable decisions, so control is credible — not assumed. Compliance is built into how data moves, not generated after the fact.
