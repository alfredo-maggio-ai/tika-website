What It's Like Here

Small team. Hard problems.
High stakes.

You'll own real things

This isn't a place where work disappears into a backlog. What you build ships. You'll see the impact of your decisions on customers who depend on it.

The problem is genuinely hard

Cryptographic data controls that work at enterprise scale, across AI systems, without breaking workflows — this hasn't been solved. That's why we're here.

No performance theater

We don't do status meetings for status's sake or roadmaps designed to look busy. We work, we ship, we learn. If you need structure around that, we're probably not a fit.

You'll be heard

We're small enough that your instincts matter. If you see something wrong — technically, architecturally, strategically — say so. We expect that.
